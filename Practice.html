What are scrunchies?

Scrunchies are a band that hold back one's hair. These can be made of all different types of materials, however, they are always shaped round.

Scrunchies in the 80's

Scrunchies in the 80's were a hit. People wore all kinds of bright neon colors. Glow in the dark scrunchies were really popular.

Scrunchies in the 90's

Scrunchies went from rebellious in the 80's to a classic look in the 90's. Colors were more subtle in the late 90's and fabrics like velvet were introduced.

Scrunchies Today

Scrunchies have a strong hold on hair culture still this day. And there is lots of self-expression in scrunchie wearing and the boundaries around scrunchie wearing continue to be knocked down.
